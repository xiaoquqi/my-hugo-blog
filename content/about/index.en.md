## Profile

Starting from a telecom-grade GSM network management system, through Motorola's global handset launch configuration platform, PaaS cloud development, and OpenStack private cloud delivery — a twenty-year journey always focused on **enterprise infrastructure software**, evolving from engineering to product leadership.

Founded a startup in 2013, secured **SoftBank China Series A investment in 2020**, and led the design and development of three core products: cloud-native DR platform **HyperBDR**, online cloud migration platform **HyperMotion**, and enterprise AI model operations platform **AGIOne**. Products are deployed in Saudi Arabia, Mexico, Malaysia, Brazil, Nigeria, and beyond — serving government judicial bodies, major banks, and Global 500 manufacturers, including **3,700 VMs migrated in 3 months** and **70 TB of judicial data protected with zero margin for error**. Throughout global expansion, I've been hands-on across the full commercial cycle — **pre-sales solution demos, technical due diligence, and post-delivery support** — conducting all business negotiations and technical presentations directly in English with clients across Saudi Arabia, Mexico, Malaysia, Brazil, and beyond.

As one of **17 official Ceph Foundation Ambassadors worldwide**, I've spent over a decade building China's Ceph community and advancing open-source distributed storage. I co-founded the Ceph China Community, organized **Cephalocon APAC 2018** — Ceph's first-ever global summit — and have spoken at multiple OpenStack and Ceph Asia-Pacific summits, bringing China's open-source voice to the world stage.

Long-running technical education and content work: custom OpenStack training for Ericsson, HP, Cisco, Inspur, and others; published courses on CSDN and 51CTO; and an active Douyin tech channel with **10,000+ followers**, focused on cloud computing and AI trends. Making complex technology teachable and systematic is a habit baked into how I work.

In the AI era, the push is toward the **full replacement** of traditional development with AI across the team: shipped products built with **100% AI-driven development**, systematically tackled the hallucination and reliability challenges that arise in production AI applications, significantly boosted overall team efficiency through AI tooling, and continuously expanded the company's product portfolio powered by AI. At the organizational level, exploring a new engineering paradigm for the AI era — from knowledge sharing to workflow redesign — driving the team's evolution toward a human-AI collaboration culture.
